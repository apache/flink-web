Flink In Production

Real-world Flink Use Cases

To demonstrate Flink's capabilities, we've included a few examples of Flink running in production at large enterprises. In addition, the Powered by Flink page provides a comprehensive list of companies and organizations who are using Flink.

Alibaba, the world's largest retailer, uses a Flink-based system to optimize search rankings in realtime. See their talk from Hadoop Summit 2016 San Jose here. Capital One, a Fortune 500 financial services company, uses Flink for real-time customer activity monitoring. See their case study slides from Flink Forward 2015 here.
King, the creators of Candy Crush Saga, uses Flink to provide data science teams with real-time analytics dashboards. Read about their implementation at the King Tech Blog. Zalando, one of the top 5 largest ecommerce companies in Europe, uses Flink for real-time business process monitoring and continuous ETL. Read about their evaluation process and implementation at the Zalando Tech Blog.
