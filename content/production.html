Powered by Flink

To demonstrate Flink's capabilities, we've included a few examples of how Flink is being used at large enterprises. Check out the Powered by Flink directory for a comprehensive list of companies and organizations using Flink.

Alibaba, the world's largest retailer, built a Flink-based system (Blink) to optimize search rankings in real time.

See Alibaba's talk from Hadoop Summit 2016 San Jose. 		Bouygues Telecom uses Flink for real-time event processing over billions of Kafka messages per day.

Read about stream processing at Bouygues. 		Capital One, a Fortune 500 financial services company, uses Flink for real-time customer activity monitoring.

See Capital One's case study slides from Flink Forward 2015.
King, the creators of Candy Crush Saga, uses Flink to provide data science teams with real-time analytics.

Read about their implementation at the King Tech Blog. 		Otto Group, the world's 2nd-largest online retailer, uses Flink for business intelligence stream processing.

Read about their implementation and evaluation process. 		Zalando, one of the largest ecommerce companies in Europe, uses Flink for real-time business process monitoring.

Read about their implementation at the Zalando Tech Blog.
